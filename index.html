Micah The Cat

About Micah

Micah was not only a cat, he was a friend. He took the little time he had to spend with my family and I. We do not know how he did it, but he managed to be there for all of us without anyone feeling left out.

Micah's Characteristics

  1. Shiny white and grey coat.
  2. Pink little nose and toes.
  3. Vocal about the way he feels.
  4. Compassionate.
